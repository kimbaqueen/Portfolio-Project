Wage Gap

On average women earn 83¢ for
every $1 earned by men.

However when comparing demographics:
  • Black women earned 64¢
  • Latinas earned 57¢
  • White women earned 79¢
Working Parents

Women do more unpaid work than Men
  • U.S. Mothers average 14 Hrs on childcare & 18 hrs on household duties per week
  • U.S. Fathers average 8 Hrs on childcare & 10 hrs on household duties per week
  • Paternity leave can lead to more equity at home
In Management

Women Represent 47% of U.S labor force,

but only 40.9% of women are in management roles
  • 4.3% are Black women
  • 4.3% are Latinas
  • 2.7% are Asian women
Hi! I’m Kim.
I’m a first generation, everything.
Growing up, I often had to pave my own way forward simply because we didn’t know better. My parents did the best they could, but with limited education, language and culture barriers, it was challenging to say the least. Still, I was lucky. I was loved. I was encouraged to work hard. I am determined. I took a lot of detours to get to where I am today, and the journey never ends.
But what if we could do better?

I believe we can and aim to create a community to advocate for those who are underrepresented or at risk of being left behind. May this be a starting place to challenge assumptions, bridge communities & generations, and help others become empowered to find their way too.

Let’s connect! Follow along to see what’s coming next!
